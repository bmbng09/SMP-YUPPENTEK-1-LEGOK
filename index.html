ABOUT US

SMP YUPPENTEK 1 LEGOK

SMP Yuppentek 1 Legok is a dedicated educational institution committed to shaping outstanding students with strong character and broad knowledge. Since its establishment, our school has been a home for students who strive for excellence in both academic and non-academic fields.

With professional educators and a supportive learning environment, we continuously innovate to provide the best educational experience. Our vision is to nurture a generation that is not only intellectually capable but also possesses strong moral values and is ready to face future challenges.

Through various flagship programs, we aim to offer a holistic and high-quality education, ensuring that every student has the opportunity to reach their fullest potential.
